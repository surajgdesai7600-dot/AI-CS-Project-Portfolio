AI-Powered Customer Support Automation

Transforming Customer Service with Generative AI

An initiative to integrate GPT with Freshdesk and Freshchat, drastically reducing customer wait times and empowering the support team to handle complex issues.

The Challenge: A Support Bottleneck

Our support system faced significant strain, impacting both customer satisfaction and agent morale. We identified three core problems.

Long Wait Times

Customers with simple questions often waited over 15 minutes for a first response, leading to frustration.

Agent Overload

Support agents were overwhelmed with repetitive queries, causing burnout and diverting focus from complex cases.

No Self-Service

A lack of an effective, immediate channel for customers to resolve common issues on their own.

My Role: Product Analyst & Project Lead

As the lead from the Product Analytics side, I was responsible for the end-to-end strategy and execution of this high-impact initiative.

Customer Journey Design

Mapped the entire user flow, from initial chat interaction to final resolution or human escalation.

KPI Definition

Defined and tracked key success metrics: response time, deflection rate, and customer satisfaction (CSAT).

Stakeholder Collaboration

Coordinated with Engineering, Support, Marketing, and Legal teams to ensure a seamless and compliant rollout.

Performance Monitoring

Built and maintained performance dashboards in Freshdesk Analytics to track the bot's effectiveness and guide iterations.

The Solution: A Step-by-Step Breakdown

We adopted a modern Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) approach. Think of it as giving the AI an "open book" test, where the book only contains our company's approved information, ensuring it never gives a wrong or made-up answer.

How the AI Chatbot Works

1. Building the "Brain"

We gathered all official documents‚Äîpolicies, FAQs, and past support tickets. After cleaning up outdated information, we organized it all into a structured Knowledge Base. This is the single source of truth for the AI.

2. Understanding the Question

When a customer asks a question, our system doesn't send it directly to the AI. First, it searches our Knowledge Base to find the *exact snippets* of information relevant to the query.

3. Crafting the Answer

Only then do we pass the customer's question AND the relevant snippets to GPT. We instruct it: "Answer this question using only the information I've provided." This ensures the response is accurate and adheres strictly to our policies.

Smart Escalation: The Safety Net

To ensure a great experience, we built a safety net. After the AI generates an answer, our system calculates a confidence score. If the score is high (above 75%), the answer is sent to the customer instantly. If the score is low, meaning the AI is not confident, it doesn't guess. Instead, it automatically replies, "Let me connect you to a support specialist," and creates a ticket for a human agent. This guarantees that complex or unusual queries always get human attention.

Key Learnings

  • Grounding is crucial. AI's value is only realized when grounded in an accurate, up-to-date knowledge base.
  • Adoption is intentional. The visibility and placement of a chatbot directly impact its usage and success.
  • Analytics closes the loop. Continuous monitoring is essential to prove impact and guide iterative improvements.
